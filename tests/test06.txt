## DESC
-f flattens arrays, skips missing elements

## ARGS
-f uid addresses.1.zip name

## INPUT
{"uid":1001,"name":"John Doe","addresses":[{"street":"123 Main St","zip":"12345"}, {"street":"303 2nd St", "zip": "33112"}]}
{"uid":1002,"name":"Jane Doe","addresses":[{"street":"456 Elm St","city":"Othertown","zip":"67890"}]}
{"uid":1003,"name":"Meesha Jones"}
{"uid":1004,"name":"Bob Smith","addresses":[{"street":"789 Maple St","city":"Sometown","zip":"45678"},{"street":"1015 Folsom", "city": "SF", "zip": "94103-4016"}]}

1002                   Jane Doe
