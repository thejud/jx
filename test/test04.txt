## DESC
-f flattens with dot notation

## ARGS
-f uid address.zip

## INPUT

{"uid":1001,"name":"John Doe","address":{"street":"123 Main St","city":"Anytown","zip":"12345"}}
{"uid":1002,"name":"Jane Doe","address":{"street":"456 Elm St","city":"Othertown","zip":"67890"}}

## OUTPUT

uid   address.zip
1001  12345
1002  67890
